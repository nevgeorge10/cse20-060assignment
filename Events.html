Dubai Events

DUBAI SHOPPING FESTIVAL

Dates: 17th December 2020 to 30th January 2021

With crazy sales and unbelievable discounts running across major brands, the Dubai Shopping Festival secures the top spot on our list of Dubai’s biggest annual events.

DUBAI JAZZ FESTIVAL

Dates: To be announced

Held at the Dubai Media City Amphitheatre, Dubai Jazz Festival is another highly anticipated music festivals in Dubai.

NATIONAL DAY CELEBRATION

2nd December

Our final feature is one of the most anticipated annual celebrations in Dubai – the UAE National Day, which falls on the 2nd of December.

DUBAI SUMMER SURPRISES

Dates: 24th June to 4th September 2021

The Dubai Summer Surprises (DSS) is a sales extravaganza that takes place right after the schools close for the summer break.

DUBAI FOOD FESTIVAL

Dates: 25th March to 17th April 2021

To all the foodies out there, this is one of the annual festivals in Dubai that should be on your calendar. Dubai Food Festival visitors can enjoy dining at some of the most incredible restaurants and enjoy exceptional discounted deals.

DUBAI FITNESS CHALLENGE

Dates: 29th October to 27th November 2021

The purpose of the event is to challenge residents to opt for a healthier lifestyle by participating in 30 minutes of exercise for 30 consecutive days.
